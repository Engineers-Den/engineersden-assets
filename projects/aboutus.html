Who We Are

A multidisciplinary platform for innovation, prototyping, and applied learning.

Engineer’s Den currently operates in three major areas: R&D services, product design and development, and science club activities. We provide R&D support in electronics-related fields, supply lab-grade components and equipment, design products in-house, and support student projects through prototyping, 3D printing, and laser cutting.

What defines us

We combine technical problem-solving with practical execution. Our work is built around designing, developing, testing, and supporting real solutions that can be used in education, research, industry, and everyday life.

From academic project support to custom business solutions, Engineer’s Den aims to make innovation more accessible, actionable, and affordable.

What makes us different

Our strength lies in connecting theory with real implementation. We do not just discuss ideas — we turn them into prototypes, products, systems, and learning experiences.

This practical mindset allows us to serve researchers, students, organizations, and general consumers under one innovation-focused brand.

Our Story

Built from collaboration, experience, and a shared vision.

Engineer’s Den was founded in May 2024 by two engineers, Md. Rakib Hossain and Fuzayel Mahmud. As long-time collaborators in electronics and applied engineering, they brought together their hands-on experience and shared vision to create a firm focused on designing products and advancing R&D in Bangladesh.

What started as a strong technical partnership has now grown into a broader platform that supports product development, innovation services, student project assistance, and science-based community activities.
